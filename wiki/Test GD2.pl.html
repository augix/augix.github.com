<perl>

use GD;
  1. create a new image $im = new GD::Image(100,100);
  1. allocate some colors $white = $im->colorAllocate(255,255,255); $black = $im->colorAllocate(0,0,0); $red = $im->colorAllocate(255,0,0); $blue = $im->colorAllocate(0,0,255);
  1. make the background transparent and interlaced $im->transparent($white); $im->interlaced('true');
  1. Put a black frame around the picture $im->rectangle(0,0,99,99,$black);
  1. Draw a blue oval $im->arc(50,50,95,75,0,360,$blue);
  1. And fill it with red $im->fill(50,50,$red);
  1. make sure we are writing to a binary stream open(STDOUT,">pic2.png"); binmode STDOUT;
  1. Convert the image to PNG and print it on standard output print $im->png;

</perl> Category:Perl
