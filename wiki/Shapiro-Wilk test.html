Shapiro-Wilk test

In statistics, the '''Shapiro-Wilk test''' tests the null hypothesis that a sample ''x''1, ..., ''x''''n'' came from a normally distributed population. It was published in 1965 by Samuel Shapiro and Martin Wilk.
