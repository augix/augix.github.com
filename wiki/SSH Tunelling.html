SSH Tunelling

Create Tunel

In <tt>~/.bashrc</tt> <bash> ## localhost:7777 -> gate -> eva001:22 alias sshrzg='ssh -l augix -L 7777:eva001.opt.rzg.mpg.de:22 gate.rzg.mpg.de cat -' alias eva='ssh -p 7777 localhost' </bash>

After typing sshrzg You forwarded localhost:7777 to eva001.opt.rzg.mpg.de:22

<tt>localhost:7777 -> eva001:22</tt>

Use Tunel

  • to connect: eva
  • to copy files

<bash> scp -P 7777 localhost:/nfs/eva/augix/cDNA/simulation/sub.sh ~/Projects/cDNA/simulation/sub.sh scp -P 7777 ~/Projects/cDNA/simulation/sub.sh localhost:/nfs/eva/augix/cDNA/simulation/sub.sh </bash>

Use <tt>Yummy FTP</tt> or any other FTP client

  • Make a bookmark for eva001.opt.rzg.mpg.de:/nfs/eva/augix/cDNA

** server: localhost ** port: 7777 ** username: augix ** password: ******** ** initial path: /nfs/eva/augix/cDNA
