What makes us human?

my answer

超大的脑袋和合作行为。互为因果。

  • 逻辑：
    • 他们是相辅相成，互为因果的。因为脑袋大而复杂，所以有了合作行为的神经基础，也有了语言和文化这种高级的东西。因为多个家族成员对繁育后代的合作和支持，所谓cooperative breading，才有了大脑袋发育，所需要的食物，时间和保护。

2. 证据

  • Humans have higher social intelligence {Herrmann:2007du}.
  • Humans have bigger brain when normalized by body weight {Allman:2000ww, Roth:2005db}.
  • Great apes cannot afford bigger brains with their daily energy income {FonsecaAzevedo:2012cd}.
  • Group hunting and cooking (food processing) fulfill energy need of the big brain {Carmody:2009er}.
