augixs-ibook-g4:~ augix$ s='\t\n' augixs-ibook-g4:~ augix$ echo $s \t\n </bash>

  • concatenate two strings:

The braces are required for concatenation constructs.

<pre> $p_01 The value of the variable "p_01".

${p}_01 The value of the variable "p" with "_01" pasted onto the end. </pre>
